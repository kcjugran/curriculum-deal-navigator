# Curriculum Deal Navigator\n\nA consultant-focused negotiation, scoping, pricing, proposal, and deal-evaluation platform designed for curriculum developers, certification architects, coaches, consultants, educators, and intellectual-property-based service providers.\n\n## Overview\n\nThis is NOT a pricing calculator. This is NOT a questionnaire.\n\nThis is a **professional negotiation dashboard** that helps consultants think clearly during business conversations, avoid scope creep, determine pricing, evaluate opportunities, generate proposals, and make informed decisions.\n\n## Features\n\n### Core Functionality\n- **Pre-Call Discovery Checklist** - Modal reminder of key questions to ask before discussing pricing\n- **Unified Dashboard** - Single-screen interface with inputs on the left, outputs on the right\n- **Real-Time Updates** - All recommendations update as you input information\n- **Customizable Pricing Engine** - Flexible pricing profiles (not hard-coded)\n- **Scope Creep Detection** - Automatic alerts when deliverables exceed engagement type\n- **Negotiation Assistant** - Real-time guidance on business conversations\n- **Scenario Manager** - Save, load, duplicate, and compare scenarios\n- **Export & Proposals** - Generate PDFs and copy-ready responses\n- **Dual View Modes** - Consultant view (full strategy) and Client view (public information only)\n\n### Input Sections\n1. **Project Type** - Internal Coach Training / Commercial Certification Program\n2. **Level of Involvement** - Advisory Only / Curriculum Outline / Full Curriculum Development\n3. **Curriculum Scope** - Level 1 / Level 2 / Level 3 / Multi-Level\n4. **Client Economics** - Revenue, student volume, business outcomes\n5. **Budget Intelligence** - Budget range and notes\n6. **Relationship Strength** - Cold / Warm / Existing Client / Trusted Relationship\n7. **Ownership & Attribution** - IP ownership and attribution status\n8. **Project Complexity** - Sliders for complexity, revision risk, decision-maker involvement, timeline urgency\n9. **Deliverables** - Checkboxes for all possible deliverables\n\n### Output Sections\n- Project Summary\n- Scope Summary\n- Deliverables Summary\n- Recommended Engagement Type\n- Pricing Guidance (Ideal, Acceptable, Walk-Away)\n- Timeline Estimate\n- Attribution & IP Notes\n- Risk Analysis\n- Negotiation Guidance\n- Next Questions to Ask\n- Proposal Preview\n- Revenue Analysis\n\n## Getting Started\n\n### Prerequisites\n- Node.js 18+
